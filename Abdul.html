Abdul

Details

Abdul traveled the world of Vetras during The Ruinations along side such notable heros as Finbar, Elder Creel, Siren, Yuri, Reggie Twinkletoes, and Ronan Korokuma. He was the finisher of the events chronicled in The Testament of the Watcher. Abdul possessed the same talents as his forefathers, and was capable of seeing both the past, present, and future. Like The Prophet, Abdul was born and then cast out of his home, signifying he would be the last of his line. Abdul was considered one of theFate Watchers, though he accomplished nothing so great as his ancestor and was born in The Dark Age.
