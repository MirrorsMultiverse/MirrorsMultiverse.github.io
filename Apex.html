Apex

Backstory

A soldier who came over from the Empire to fight in the war against Saffron.

Details

After freeing Lysand, a country in Vertigonia from the queen Saffron, Apex joined Corvan in The Radiant War against the gods. He perished in the seige against the City of Cinder, slain by a member of the Gatewatch.
