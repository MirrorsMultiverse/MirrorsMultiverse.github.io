Arimathea Oxicat

Backstory

An amnesiac who woke up in an ally with a ring of multicolored keys, each one made from a different metal. She seeks to find her way in the world and discover her past.
For more information see: The Gates.

Details

Finding her way into the city of Cosmeria, she was hired by an elderly orc, along with several other of her companions, to investigate an underwater city run by a local crime lord. She has a distinct hate of cucumbers. She appeared in the Quests of Cosmeria campaign.
