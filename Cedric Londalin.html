Cedric Londalin

Backstory

Son of weak lord, he embarked on a quest to hunt down one Tormund who was besmearching his father's name.

Details

He tracks down Tormund to a small village. In this village he meets a elder who asks Cedric to help him find the source of some mysterious fog which surrounds the village and has already taken several villagers. Obligated by his oath, Cedric sets out to investigate with his two companions. One, a druid sees this forest for what it truely is. They soon get seperated and Cedric fights a Hag.
