Elerrina Starborne

Details

A crucial character in the campaign Tales of Vertigonia, Elerrina was a Druid of the High Elves. She was a traveler and a wayfinder, splitting through great wildernesses to find old forgotten temples and lost civilizations. Elerrina was also a priestess of the goddess Narria who patroned her druidic arts. She travled with Lindal Alderleaf and Primrose the Dancer for a time, before she was slain by The Black Hand alongside Primrose. She returned from the afterlife later, with an army of Dragons to fight off Lindal, now enhanced by The Watcher and an army of criminals and outlaws. She was turned to stone at the end of the battle, with everyone else in the area.
