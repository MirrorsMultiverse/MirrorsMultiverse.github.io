Elwin Althorn

Details

Elwin led thousands and thousands of soldiers alongside Corvan during the Third Breaking or The Radiant War. He crusaded for so many millennia he eventually became powerful enough to kill stars with his Sword of Light. He married the ascended goddess of creation Emma Althorn and they had two children, Lux and Creare. These children bore their parents powers, the power of light and the power of creation. Using these powers they fought and sealed away the Tarrasque as well as four titan lords. Holding them in the bowls of Vetras until Holson ProudfootThe Ruinations. Elwin was ultimately slain in a battle against the Gatewatch and his power as well as the Sword of Light was cast down onto a distant world.
