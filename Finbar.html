Finbar

Backstory

Finbar was an Elvish foot soldier of Babylon. He traveled to Kanta (Vetras) with settlers to try and make it inhabitable during the year 3979 (Vetras Reckoning).

Details

Finbar traveled with Elder Creel and Ronan Korokuma during the events of Ruination IV. He was caught in the blast of a particular powerful spell created by Elder Creel. It was this blast that turned him from an ordinary foot soldier, to a wild magic sorcerer. In Garralondo quite early in his travels he was killed by a monster, but his companions cast a golden idol, forged by the oni, into the water. This idol brought him back to life, and would bring him back, time and time again. In an altercation with a general of Nineveh, Finbar created another wild magic sorcerer by the name of Krakena. Finbar was lost at sea shortly afterwards and he washed up on the shore of Nineveh with nothing but his fey companion Syl to guide him. The royal family of Nineveh took him in as one of their own, and he found love with the young Princess Rosa. After fleeing from Krakena for a time, Finbar approached him in battle, and each slew the other with the weakness they shared.
