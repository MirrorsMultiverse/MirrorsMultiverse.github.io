Godric

Backstory

Godric was a Camelot soldier who perished in a battle against the armies of Zilphren. Instead of passing on to death, he fell into The Dark World and gained the curse of undeath. He then traveled through those lands, slaughtering monsters and hordes of the mindless undead, those ghouls and zombies.

Details

During the events of Ruination IV Godric meets the newly undead Ronan Korokuma as he flees the cursed tomb the Oni have made for him. They travel through the land of the living or Vetras and also through The Dark World on their journey. Godric eventually helped Ronan take the imperial city of Zilphren from its corrupt monarch and restore the throne to the rightful heir, or Ronan.
