Grimble Glorfin

Backstory

Grimble has been sailing the seas in his private craft for most of his life and had never experienced anything supernatural until one day an enormous storm hit while he was out at sea. During the storm he sees a ship and boards it hoping to loot it while the occupants are sheltering from the storm. On the ship he meets Elwin Althorn and Corvan. They hed to the nearest town in search of shelter, which happens to be Port.

Details

He follows Corvan in his quest to overthrow the gods, which at first Grimble did not believe in. Unlike Corvan and Elwin though he does not seek power and remains mortal as Corvan and Elwin ascend to godhood status. After visiting the Tree of Light and the Reality and ultimately defeating Azathoth along with Corvan and Elwin, Grimble chooses to forget his adventures and return to being a sailor while Corvan and Elwin travel throughout the universe on grand quests.
