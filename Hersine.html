Hersine

Details

Following the steady decline of the Gatewatch during the latter years of The Radiant War, Hersine would abandon his post at Tegul Ana after his superiors allowed it to fall. He would go on to seek out Elder Creel, and any of his remaining allies as a last ditch effort to follow through on his order's promise to help them in their time of need. Finding Creel dead alongside Finbar following their departure from Tegul Ana, Hersine would ally himself with Reggie Twinkletoes during the final days of The Ruinations (See Ruination IV campaign for more details). After fleeing Vetras to escape the coming fate of The Perfection, Hersine would later return with Reggie to salvage whatever remained in the wreckage of the fourth ruination. However, being wounded by Jasher under control of the Perfection would later start to take its toll. After attempting to destroy a Phyrexian beast in the destruction ridden swamps of Kanta (Vetras), he would slowly start to succumb to it's influences.
Hersine would later take his own life following Reggie's death at the hand of the Phyrexians, refusing to allow the perfection to overtake him.
