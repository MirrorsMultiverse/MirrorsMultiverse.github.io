Hollow

Details

Ort accompanied Holson Proudfoot and Ralof during the events of Call of Cthulhu. The knight and protector of the princess Hedge. He was enchanted by the princess so that he could perform transmutation magics. Hollow was an empty vessel containing the soul of a human before he was given a body by Hedge. At one point he pledged his service to Holson Proudfoot and then again to Ralof, but his loyalties always remained bound to his people and princess, first and foremost. After Hedge died at the beginning of The Radiant War, Hollow returned to Holson and offered his assistance to him.
