Horace Winthrop

Backstory

Horace grew up in the streets of Death. He learned to fend for himself from a young age. One day, after a particularly deft escape, he was approached by an old man. The old man admonished him, saying that he was destined for something greater. The old man proceeded to mutter an incantation, binding Horace to a young Dwarven prince named Bregor. The old man then charged Horace with the protection of Bregor. He was to guard him with his life and never reveal Bregor's royal origins until the king died. Horace was stunned, but felt a strong compulsion to do what the old man commanded. He eventually grew to love Bregor, and relish his duty as protector.

Details

Lived in the world of Astarius. Friend and guardian of Bregor. He witnessed the discovery of Green Adamentine, and rediscovered the Gates of Oblivion. He later died at the hands of Dawnrunners. Horace's link to the Shadowfell and the wish he made the Djinn before he died, animated his reflection to bring him back as a specter.
