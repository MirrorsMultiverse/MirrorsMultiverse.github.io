Jasher

Backstory

Jasher was a nomad of the deserts of Nineveh.

Details

After the fall of Krakena, he entered into the city and was elected by Princess Rosa to lead Regie Twinkletoes and Elder Creel through the desert to the Prophet's Tomb in Nineveh, and then Camelot. He did so and the fought through hordes of stone warriors amd undead to find clues to cure the Bloodborne. After he survived the final wave of the Bloodborne, he took the cured warriors of Wartorn and mustered an army of 2,000,000 men.

With this army, Reggie, and one Hersine, he marched upon the capital of Camelot to kill Necron or perhaps win him over. Necron deceived them all and created a powerful charm that caused the army to turn on itself, many of Hersine's legion were lost. Upon entering the capital and attempting to kill Necron, the Tarrasque came and attacked the city. Jasher survived but his arm and leg were destroyed by its lazer breath. They were later regenerated by Syl. He then travled to The Edge and fought the Phyrexians in the final battle of The Ruinations. He later married one Marria.
