Lakota

Details

A Wolfkin of Vertigonia. He travled with Narria during the days she sought to learn the lives of mortals. He was born and died in The Age of Creation. Lakota was the first being to ever fight and slay an incarnation of The Watcher, he also slew Dragons so that their blood fell like rain. He was called, the Devouring Hero, King of Blades and Claws.
