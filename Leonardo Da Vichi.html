Leonardo Da Vichi

Backstory

It was an ordinary day in the city for Leonardo, until his assistant Pip brought a mysterious stranger by the name of Versmir Templar and a crate of illegal elixirs into the shop. This single event completely derailed everything Leonardo had worked for. Once word was out nothing could stop them, legionnaires spilled into the streets around emporium trashing the entire tavern and ruining Leonardo's livelihood. He had fled with one Bregan Greeves and sought to avoid arrest by the legionaries. This was until a riveting court hearing was swayed to his favor granting him immense social status and favor of Alistar. This power he holds now could prove vital for restoring his business to its former glory and beyond.

Details

He seeks to out-bake the Gourmet, his arch nemesis, and once possible completely wipe their name from the face of Vertigonia. He is determined to receive payment and compensation for his damaged business from all who greaved him, namely Versmir Templar. Aside from that he really just seems to want to bake an unhealthy amount of pizza. He has visions of Yog-Sothoth and Versmir Templar in his waking dreams.
