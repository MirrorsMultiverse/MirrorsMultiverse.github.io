Lotus

Details

The wife of Pharoah in Vetras. One of Lotus's eyes could force someone to do her will, and the other eye could steal the soul of a creature. Lotus was one of the most powerful godskin humans, but she was eventually killed by Princess Rosa of the West. Lotus gave Rosa the secret to resurrection and seven dark enchantments so that her children would be spared.
