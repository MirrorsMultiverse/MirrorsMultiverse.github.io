Narria

Details

One of the goddesses of creation in Vertigonia. She patroned many celrics and paladins. With a kind and caring passion, she guards the beasts of the field. She patroned Ralof during the events of the Link and Elerrina Starborne during Tales of Vertigonia. Not even she was spared during the wrath of the The Radiant War. Narria had two sisters, Nirranor and Natrinas, of which she is the youngest of the three. Narria was a special deity because she was the only one to ever willfully give up her divinity, during the The Age of Creation she came down to the mortal worlds. She travled over all the land with Lakota, seeking to find what the lives of those she watched from the heavens were really like. She fought beasts and slew monsters, directly helping those she was forbidden to assist from the skies.
