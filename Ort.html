Ort

Backstory

Pulled from a pit by Holson Proudfoot, Ort swore to serve and protect his rescuer.

Details

Ort accompanied Holson Proudfoot and Ralof during the events of Call of Cthulhu. He also travelled with two great wolves, one by the name of The Watcher and the other Vivianna. Ort eventually fought against Cthulhu and went on to inherit a dwarfish crown in Vertigonia in the kingdom of Barrowdeep. He married the human Trix and, as she is human and he dwarf, embarked on a trek across Vertigonia seeking the Lifestone. As he got up in years he felt he must make a impact of the world and not let old deeds lie forgotten. He gathered all his old journals and scrolls he wrote during his journeys and, with the help of Holson, comprised a epic compilation of stories known as The Celestrianth. This book contains a epic recount of the trek for the Lifestone among many other things. A common folk tale roughly based on this account is known as the Epic of Gilgamesh.
