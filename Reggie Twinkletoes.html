Reggie Twinkletoes

Backstory

A half leprechaun brought up in the Abby of Ravenloft. He trained under Father _ as a monk until one day he was sent by the abby to seek out information about the Red Gate. He was changed when Elder Creel cast a particularly powerful spell and grew an affinity to earth. He meets up with Elder Creel and Finbar on the beach and experiences the Red Gate opening.

Details

Appears in the Ruination IV campaign with Elder Creel and Finbar and later when Finbar dies, Jasher. He was guided throughout his journey by his morals, and also a fey spirit called Syl. Reggie believed he was marked to cure the Bloodborne, and marked he was. Early on in his journey he killed an oni, setting himself directly against the dark god Orucus. He traveled through the land of Nineveh and then Camelot searching for a cure. Eventually he learned that someone must be truly selfless and absorb all illness, in all the land, simply by committing self sacrifice. Elder Creel, learning of this, took Reggie's place. Reggie later met a brutal end when he was slain by an Obliterator of the Phyrexians. The only witness to his death was one Hersine, who died immediately after the encounter. Reggie's likeness was later cast into a great statue of marble by Princess Rosa, and over the decades a forest grew up around it. It was this forest that allowed for the fey to return to Vetras. The sacrifice and arcana of Reggie became a gate to the feywilds. The statue stands to this day, though covered in foliage, it is unbreakable, and it is guarded by earth elementals. Later when The Festering began, any man or woman who took a pilgrimage to the ancient statue and gazed upon it, would be healed.
