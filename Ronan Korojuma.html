Ronan Korokuma

Backstory

Details

Part of the Ruination IV campaign along with Elder Creel and Finbar. He dies at The Edge, slain by a monster of the dragons named Cockatrice, but is later resurrected by his squire Yuri and gains the Hallow race. It was the Ancient Dragon Larsinax who ordered his death. Later on, the Phoenix choses him to be a herald of good and justice in the world, as well as the emperor of mankind. Ronan then led an army of dragons against the false emperor and took the throne of Zilphren, rebuilding the world after the wreckage of The Ruinations. He also traveled for a time in the company of Siren, Godric, and Abdul, all of whom helped him rebuild after The Ruinations. He was later killed by Princess Lilith in her war against the surface world.
