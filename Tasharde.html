Tasharde

Backstory

A Djinn, stolen from his tribe as a child by human raiders, Tasharde was brought up as a warriors trained to be a gladiator. Where most children had toys, he had daggers. When most played with cats, he killed lions. Djinn are already a savage race, with the habit of mangling the bodies of their enemies after death, but Tasharde was merciless. Before meeting Olaf Stormcloak and Hadvar Stormcloak he would frequently assassinate noblmen and their honor guards as revenge for his slain kin. Djinn dream of reclaiming the deserts of Lysand for themselves, as it has been once.

Details

Tasharde guided Olaf and Hadvar through the deserts on a quest to find their brother. Rumors of one Saffron came, a queen of great might to take the deserts and perhaps, the world. Tasharde turned his efforts to finding and killing her. He was slain shortly after making this resolution by a sort of desert wraith. While his mortal body perished there, his spirit lived on and he became a Genie or a Djinn spirit of air and fire. As a Genie, Tasharde sought even more to prevent humans from gaining power in the world. And so, with his new ability to read the future , he sought out a weakling who would soon rise to power. The Genie challenged Corvan for Arodonite when the Lady of the Lake first presented it to him. Corvan banished the Genie that was Tasharde's spirit back to the Elemental Plain after the Lady of the Lake applauded his courage and presented him with the sword regardless of his failure to originally defeat the Genie.
