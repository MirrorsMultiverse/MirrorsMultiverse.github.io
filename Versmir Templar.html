Versmir Templar

Backstory

Vehersmir was an imperial prince, betrothed to the princess of the emperor. He spent his days practicing swordplay and learning arts such as wrestling, medicine, and smithing. At the age of twenty five he traveled with his lady, family, fellow soldiers, and a great many friends to the old emperial colonies of Vertigonia. Just off the coast however, they were attacked unprovoked and sunk by Legionnaires. His family and friends were slaughtered, and he was slain and cast into the sea. Oddly enough, the Great Old Ones had other plans. Washed up on a strange land, he is guided to destroy that which he knows, by that which he knows not. Once an emperial knight and prince, now a lowly vagabond.

Details

His patron is Yog-Sothoth, and the Great Old One uses his thrall to accomplish his will in the world. Every life taken by his Hexblade "Sheol" is another soul devoured by Yog-Sogoth.
